Authorization Agreement for Preauthorized Payments (Aqua Pay)

Sign up for automatic payments from either your checking or savings account. With AquaPay, your balance due will be automatically drawn each month on the due date of your bill. You will still receive a bill in the mail (or by e-mail for E-Bill customers) for your records showing the charges each month, but once enrolled you will never need to write a check to us again!
